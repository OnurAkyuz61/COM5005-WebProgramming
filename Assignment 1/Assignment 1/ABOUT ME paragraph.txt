I am a computer engineer which is a broad word under which there are many job roles. As a computer engineer I would be responsible for developing, designing, testing and implementing various computer based software and hardware, which includes certain components found inside the computer equipment. 
Furthermore, I can care for the devices within the computer and the software which power these machines. Finally, I can design the network and assure its security by integrating various software programs and hardware. Computer engineers are in heavy demand all through the business sectors in the world.
